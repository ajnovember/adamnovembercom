about me
I'm passionate about music, technology, and education. I enjoy building instruments, playing guitar with lots of pedals, drawing with javascript, and many other things.

I graduated from NYU's music technology program in 2014 with a minor in computer science. Since then, I've been teaching computer science and music technology for various schools and programs around the city, including:
  • Montclair State University
  • New York University
  • Urban Arts Partnership
  • The Lang School
  • Manhattan Youth
You can see my full resume here. I also play guitar in band called Oxymorrons and help organize the Monthly Music Hackathon NYC.
