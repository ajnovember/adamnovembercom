Groove Pizza
Groove Pizza is a circular rhythm interface that I worked on as a research assistant at the NYU Music Experience Design Lab. I worked alongside Ethan Hein, the original creator, to design and develop the first digital prototype in JavaScript, shown in the above video.

Groove Pizza is designed to be a playful intro to many of the more advanced ideas music makers will encounter in full DAWs, as well as a tool to help students visualize rhythm and relate geometric concepts to music. Development continued after I graduated, and in April 2016 the US Dept. of Education debuted the first public release of the app. View the app here.
