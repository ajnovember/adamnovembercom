Hip Hop Hacks
Hip Hop Hacks is an event that I organized and ran alongside Jonathan Marmor and Sommer McCoy as part of the Monthly Music Hackathon NYC series. The event, also cohosted byThe Young Hackers took place on April 2nd and 3rd, 2016 at the Spotify offices in Manhattan. We over 500 participants between the two days, many of whom were students. The event included musical performances, speakers ranging from Grammy winning producers to educators, workshops ranging from turntable scratching to data visualization, and lots of hacking collaborations. My responsibilities incluced booking speakers, organzing workshops (and teaching one), and emceeing the event... Planning talks for Hip Hop Hacks 2017 are currently underway!
