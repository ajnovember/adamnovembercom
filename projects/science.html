Science of Music
Science of Music was an educational video series produced within the NYU Music Technology program. The videos explored ranging topics in music technology and highlighted interesting student work within the program. Our goal was to pique interest in engineering and music technology among high school students. I wrote scripts, helped setup and run shoots, and sometimes acted on camera.
