Bud Light Music
In 2016 I joined a crew from Novalia at South by Southwest to create a series of interactive sound installations for Bud Light Music. We used capactive sensing on conductive ink paper circuits to turn posters into Bluetooth MIDI controllers and instruments. Our installations included a 50 foot playable sound wall on Brazzos Street and a silent disco where the music was live remixed by the participants, among other things. My responsibilities included soldering circuit boards that would control audio and lights, using MAX MSP to program our lights, physically constructing the poster walls, and constructing Ableton Live sets that allowed participants to control with the music they were hearing.
